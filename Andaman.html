Andaman Island
Nature's Comfort

The Andaman Islands, located in the Bay of Bengal, are a tropical paradise and a popular destination for tourism. Known for their pristine white-sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and lush greenery, the islands offer a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. The main attractions include Havelock Island, famous for its Radhanagar Beach often ranked among the world's best beaches, and Neil Island, known for its coral reefs and diverse marine life.

Day 1

Port Blair

Arrive in Port Blair, report to the coach and proceed to hotel. In the evening visit Cellular Museum and Cellular Jail, which stood mute witness to the torture meted out to the freedom fighters, who were incarcerated in this Jail. History awakens as we visit 'Veer Savarkar' Smarak and see the light and sound show.

Day 2

Swaraj Dweep

Visit Elephant Beach - very famous for the variety of untouched corals and rich marine life with diverse species. The coral reefs of this beach start about a meter or two. A little deep into the sea you can spot vibrant corals and schools of colorful fish swimming around elegantly, must admit it is an amazing view! One can enjoy various water sports on own. Enjoy get-together with your travel companions in the evening

Day 3

Laxmanpur Beach - the longest beach on Neil Island

A natural rock formation in the shape of a bridge is popularly known as Natural Bridge of Neil Island. Over a period of thousands of years, the living corals of the sea have formed this spectacular structure. Later proceed to Port Blair by Makruzz / ferry.

Day 4

Departure From Port

As your enchanting Andaman tour reaches its conclusion, the farewell at the port is a poignant moment, echoing with the echoes of azure waters and swaying palms. Reflect on the memories woven amidst the sun-kissed beaches and thriving coral gardens. Depart with a sense of fulfillment, leaving the tranquil islands behind, yet carrying the vibrant spirit of Andaman with you. Your journey concludes at the port, a gateway between the serenity of Andaman and the anticipation of new adventures.
