Kerala
Luxurious Experience

Discover the natural beauty and cultural richness of Kerala, where lush landscapes meet vibrant traditions.

Day 1

Arrival and Beach Relaxation

Arrive in Kerala and check into your accommodation near the coast, such as Varkala or Kovalam. Spend the day relaxing on the beautiful sandy beaches, soaking in the sun, and enjoying the calming sound of the waves.

Day 2

Backwater Cruise and Fort Kochi

Start the day with a scenic backwater cruise through the tranquil network of rivers and canals. Visit Fort Kochi, a historic neighborhood known for its colonial architecture and vibrant art scene.

Day 3

Wildlife Safari and Hill Station

Embark on a wildlife safari in Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Enjoy a nature walk or boat ride on Periyar Lake. Head to Munnar, a charming hill station famous for its tea plantations and breathtaking scenery.

Day 4

Munnar Exploration

Explore the scenic beauty of Munnar, visiting attractions like Tea Museum, Mattupetty Dam, and Echo Point. Experience the tranquility of nature with a visit to Eravikulam National Park.

Day 5

Cultural Experience and Departure

Immerse yourself in Kerala's rich culture with a visit to a traditional Kathakali dance performance. Explore local markets for souvenirs and depart with cherished memories.
